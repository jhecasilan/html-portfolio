My All Time Favorite Movies

The Top 3 Movies according to me

Black Hawk Down

The movie perfectly builds up the tension from the very beginning and when things finally happen, they never stopped.
It's quite a long movie but I didn't get bored. I could watch all over again.

The Grand Budapest Hotel

This movie by Wes Anderson has gripped my attention ever since the movie started.
It has a nice blend of quirkiness and thrilling scenes that are typical of the director's style.
It's always fun to see the same set of favorite actors that are always present of Anderson's movies

Pirates

This little piece of korean cinema had me rolling with laughter. I didn't expect to like this kind of silly slapstick comedy but It really had me cracking
I just hope the sequel was as funny as the first one.
